Little Heroes has been in operation since 2015. Little Heroes was a long time dream of mine that came true with the birth of my daughter. I worked for the health department and struggled to find a daycare that offered both a home environment, and a place we felt comfortable and safe leaving our most prized possession in. My husband is a Paramedic and I am a trained First Responder. Above all, we wanted to ensure our child would be safe while away from us. This began the end of our search for child care and the start of Little Heroes Home Child Care. We are so thankful for all the little ones that have come into our lives and home, and look forward to caring for many more.

Child Safety

Child safety is our top priority here at Little Heroes. While accidents can happen anywhere, we want parents to know they have the best team caring for their loved ones.
